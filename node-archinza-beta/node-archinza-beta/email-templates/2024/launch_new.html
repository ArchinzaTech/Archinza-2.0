We’re so excited to announce the second edition of BEAUTY&YOU!

Created by Estée Lauder Companies’ New Incubation Ventures and launched in partnership with NYKAA, BEAUTY&YOU aims to fuel the next generation of Indian beauty entrepreneurs with the capital, mentorship, resources and key partners that they need to build their dream brand.

This year, we’re focusing on the theme of Experiential Beauty in India and we encourage founders and creators with a fresh take on this exciting concept to apply for once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to bring your vision to life!

WHO IS IT FOR?

Founders, creatives and innovators with a unique perspective on this year’s theme of Experiential Beauty are welcome to apply as well as any applicants from 2022.

GROW

In-market Beauty Concepts in the categories of Skincare, Make Up, Hair Care, Personal Fragrance and Home Fragrance
